# Slice and Explore

> This article overviews how to query metrics and slices

The Slice and Explore Metrics question lets you answer the "What" questions about your data. You can get to this question by [searching your data](/articles/6927253-using-zenlytic#h_f10aeb1456), [clicking on a metric](/articles/6927253-using-zenlytic#h_16be460248), [following up from a dashboard](/articles/6927253-using-zenlytic#h_458995a971), or [following up from chat](/articles/6927253-using-zenlytic#h_0e78591cea).

Saving your question to a dashboard

Save your Slice and Explore question to an existing or new dashboard by clicking the 3 dots on the upper right hand side of the page next to the 'Run Question' button.
